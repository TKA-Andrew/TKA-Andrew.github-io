Artificial Intelligence - General Notes

7 types of AI

  • Narrow AI:
    • Also known as artificial narrow intelligence (ANI) or weak AI
    • Designed to complete very specific actions; unable to independently learn.
    • Often utilize machine learning and neural network algorithms to complete these specified tasks.
  • Artificial General Intelligence:
    • Designed to learn, think and perform at similar levels to humans.
  • Artificial Superintelligence:
    • Able to surpass the knowledge and capabilities of humans.
  • Reactive Machine AI:
    • Capable of responding to requests and tasks in real time
    • Unable to build memory or store information for future.
    • Can only respond to a limited combination of inputs
    • Reactive Machine AI Example: Netflix Recommendation Engine
  • Limited Memory AI:
    • AI that can store past data and use that data to make predictions..
    • The core of limited memory AI is deep learning, which imitates the function of neurons in the human brain.
    • Limited Memory AI Examples:
      • Chatbots and Virtual Assistants
      • Self-Driving Cars
  • Theory of Mind AI:
    • AI that can sense and respond to human emotions, plus perform the tasks of limited memory machines.
  • Self-Aware AI:
    • AI that can recognize othersâ€™ emotions, plus has sense of self and human-level intelligence; the final stage of AI.
